The Garden of Emotions

Information

So for this it's an emotional driven art website that uses flowers as paintbrushes, you can click any flowers you feels like it's reflecting on your emotion at the moment and draw, this website was created for viewer to express themselves basing on the speed of the cursor. This quicker you go, the more randomly the flowers will be put which is an interesting way to portray falling leaves while the slower the viewers go, the more thicked the brush will put the flowers which can easily became colors and flat shapes to draw. I would love for the viewers to express their creativity with however way they seems fit and the more interesting they can do the better
